Research Publication of Family of Saadat Naqvi

DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.20070090

Historical and genealogical research publication documenting the Naqvi Bukhari Saadat families of Hyderabad Sindh, Agra, Firozabad, Lahore, Multan Uch Sharif and Karachi. This study includes genealogical manuscripts, lineage records, migration history, archival documentation and Ahl al-Bayt-centered spiritual traditions associated with Hazrat Jalaluddin Surkh Posh Bukhari (R.A) and Imam Ali al-Naqi (A.S).
