Religious & Historical Identity: The family is recorded as Naqvi Bukhari Sayyid, connected to Imam Ali al-Naqi al-Hadi (A.S), Hazrat Jalaluddin Surkh Posh Bukhari (R.A), and later Aal-e-Ameer and Aal-e-Nafees branches. The archive records Ahl al-Bayt devotion, Ithna Ashari foundations, Sufi affiliations, and historical migration memories.
